Our Policies

  • Membership Policy: All members must present a valid membership card upon entry. .
  • Payment Policy: Membership fees must be paid in advance. .
  • Code of Conduct: Members are expected to behave respectfully towards staff and other members..
  • Health and Safety: Members must adhere to all safety guidelines and instructions provided by the gym staff.
  • Cancellation Policy: Members can cancel their membership with a 30-day notice. .
  • Privacy Policy: We are committed to protecting your privacy. Personal information collected will be used solely for gym-related purposes and will not be shared with third parties without consent.
  • Equipment Usage: Members must wipe down equipment after use and return weights to their proper place. Report any damaged equipment to staff immediately.
  • Class Booking Policy: Group classes must be booked in advance. If you cannot attend, please cancel at least 24 hours beforehand to allow others to take your spot.
  • Locker Room Policy: Lockers are available for daily use only. Personal items should not be left overnight. The gym is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
  • Guest Policy: Members can bring guests by purchasing a guest pass. Guests must adhere to all gym policies and are the responsibility of the hosting member.
